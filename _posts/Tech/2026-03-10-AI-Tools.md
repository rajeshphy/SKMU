**[Teachable Machine](https://teachablemachine.withgoogle.com/)**

- Country: United States (Google)  
- Purpose: Designed to democratize machine learning education by allowing users to train models for image, pose, and sound recognition directly in a browser without coding.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [TensorFlow](https://www.tensorflow.org/) (open source), popular: [PyTorch](https://pytorch.org/)

**[Open Knowledge Maps](https://openknowledgemaps.org/)**

- Country: Austria  
- Purpose: Created to solve information overload in academic research by clustering scientific publications into visual knowledge maps for quick exploration of topics.  
- Open Source: Yes

**[Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/)**

- Country: United States (Allen Institute for AI)  
- Purpose: AI-powered academic search engine that analyzes citation influence, research relevance, and semantic relationships between papers.  
- Open Source: Partially open research infrastructure, alternative: [OpenAlex](https://openalex.org/) (open source), popular: [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/)

**AI2 Paper Finder**

- Country: United States (Allen Institute for AI)  
- Purpose: Developed to help researchers discover relevant scientific papers through semantic search and contextual understanding rather than simple keyword search.  
- Open Source: Partially open research infrastructure, alternative: [OpenAlex](https://openalex.org/) (open source), popular: [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/)

**[Citation Gecko](https://www.citationgecko.com/)**

- Country: United Kingdom  
- Purpose: Helps researchers explore citation networks and identify related research papers through reference connections.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/) (open source), popular: [Mendeley](https://www.mendeley.com/)

**[ChatPDF](https://www.chatpdf.com/)**

- Country: Germany  
- Purpose: Allows users to upload PDFs and interact with them conversationally to extract summaries, answers, or explanations from research papers or documents.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [Haystack](https://haystack.deepset.ai/) (open source), popular: [AskYourPDF](https://askyourpdf.com/)

**[AnswerThis](https://www.answerthis.io/)**

- Country: United Kingdom  
- Purpose: AI research assistant designed to extract concise answers from multiple academic papers and summarize scientific findings.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [Haystack](https://haystack.deepset.ai/) (open source), popular: [Elicit](https://elicit.com/)

**[Storm Genie](https://stormgenie.com/)**

- Country: United States  
- Purpose: AI ideation and brainstorming tool designed to generate structured ideas, strategies, and innovation concepts.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [OpenAssistant](https://github.com/LAION-AI/Open-Assistant) (open source), popular: [Notion AI](https://www.notion.so/product/ai)

**[Napkin AI](https://www.napkin.ai/)**

- Country: United States  
- Purpose: Converts written ideas into visual diagrams and concept structures to help communicate abstract ideas more clearly.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [Excalidraw](https://excalidraw.com/) (open source), popular: [Miro](https://miro.com/)

**[MyBib](https://www.mybib.com/)**

- Country: United Kingdom  
- Purpose: Automatically generates citations and bibliographies for academic writing across different citation styles.  
- Open Source: Partially open components, alternative: [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/) (open source), popular: [EndNote](https://endnote.com/)

**[EditGPT](https://editgpt.app/)**

- Country: United States  
- Purpose: AI writing assistant designed to edit, revise, and improve text clarity while tracking writing changes.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [LanguageTool](https://languagetool.org/) (open source), popular: [Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/)

**[Mentimeter](https://www.mentimeter.com/)**

- Country: Sweden  
- Purpose: Interactive presentation platform enabling live polls, quizzes, and real-time audience engagement in classrooms and conferences.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [AhaSlides](https://ahaslides.com/) (open source version available), popular: [Kahoot](https://kahoot.com/)

**[Bricks AI](https://www.thebricks.com/)**

- Country: United States  
- Purpose: AI productivity platform integrating spreadsheets, dashboards, and documents to organize structured data and workflows.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org/) (open source), popular: [Notion](https://www.notion.so/)

**[10MinuteMail](https://10minutemail.com/)**

- Country: United States  
- Purpose: Disposable email service used for temporary registrations while protecting personal email privacy.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [MailHog](https://github.com/mailhog/MailHog) (open source), popular: [Temp Mail](https://temp-mail.org/)

**[Claude AI](https://claude.ai/)**

- Country: United States (Anthropic)  
- Purpose: Large language model designed for safe reasoning, long-document analysis, and conversational AI tasks.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [LLaMA](https://ai.meta.com/llama/) / [Mistral](https://mistral.ai/) (open source models), popular: [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/)

**[Humanize AI Pro](https://humanizeai.pro/)**

- Country: United States  
- Purpose: Converts AI-generated text into natural human-like writing to improve readability and reduce robotic language patterns.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [LanguageTool](https://languagetool.org/) (open source), popular: [QuillBot](https://quillbot.com/)

**[Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/)**

- Country: United States (Google DeepMind)  
- Purpose: Multimodal AI system capable of reasoning across text, images, and code, integrated across the Google ecosystem.  
- Open Source: No, alternative: [LLaMA](https://ai.meta.com/llama/) / [Mistral](https://mistral.ai/) (open source models), popular: [ChatGPT](https://chat.openai.com/)

**[Orange Data Mining](https://orangedatamining.com/)**

- Country: Slovenia (University of Ljubljana)  
- Purpose: Visual programming environment for machine learning, data mining, and interactive data visualization using drag-and-drop workflows.  
- Open Source: Yes, popular comparison: [RapidMiner](https://rapidminer.com/)
