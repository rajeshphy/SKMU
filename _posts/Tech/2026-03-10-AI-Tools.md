<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Teachable Machine</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States (Google)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Created to democratize machine learning education by allowing users to train models for image, sound, and pose recognition directly in the browser without programming.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No (free educational tool)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Much easier for beginners than developer frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Open Knowledge Maps</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: Austria</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Developed to address information overload in academic research by visually clustering related papers into knowledge maps.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: Yes (open science initiative)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Unlike Google Scholar’s list of results, it visually groups related research topics.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Semantic Scholar</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States (Allen Institute for AI)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Designed to improve scientific literature search through AI-driven semantic understanding and citation analysis.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: Partially open research infrastructure</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Provides deeper citation insights compared to Google Scholar.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">AI2 Paper Finder</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States (Allen Institute for AI)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Developed to help researchers discover relevant academic papers using semantic search rather than simple keyword matching.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: Research infrastructure partly open</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: More context-aware discovery than traditional academic search engines.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Citation Gecko</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United Kingdom</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Designed to visualize citation networks and track how academic papers are interconnected.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Focuses on citation discovery rather than reference management like Zotero or Mendeley.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">ChatPDF</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: Germany</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Enables users to upload research papers or documents and interact with them through conversational questions.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Similar to AskYourPDF but optimized for quick document interaction.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">AnswerThis</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United Kingdom</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Built to extract concise answers from multiple academic papers and research sources.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: More specialized for academic queries than general AI chat assistants.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Storm Genie</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Created to support brainstorming and structured idea generation during innovation or planning sessions.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Comparable to Notion AI but more focused on ideation.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Napkin AI</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Converts written ideas into visual diagrams to simplify conceptual communication.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Similar to Miro AI but focused on instant visualization of ideas.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">MyBib AI</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United Kingdom</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Designed to automatically generate citations and bibliographies for academic writing.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: Yes (citation components)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Faster and simpler than manual citation managers like EndNote.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">EditGPT</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: AI writing assistant focused on editing, rewriting, and improving text clarity.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Similar to Grammarly but stronger in rewriting and revision tracking.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Mentimeter</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: Sweden</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Interactive presentation platform enabling live polls, quizzes, and audience participation.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: More interactive than traditional presentation tools like PowerPoint.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Bricks AI</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Productivity tool combining spreadsheets, documents, and dashboards with AI assistance.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Similar to Notion AI but stronger in structured data workflows.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">10MinuteMail</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Temporary email service designed for privacy while registering on websites.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Similar to Temp Mail with disposable email functionality.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Claude AI</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States (Anthropic)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Large language model developed with emphasis on safety, reasoning, and long-document analysis.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Stronger long-document reasoning compared to many conversational AI tools.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Humanize AI Pro</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Converts AI-generated text into more natural human-like writing.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Comparable to QuillBot but designed specifically for humanizing AI text.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Gemini</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: United States (Google DeepMind)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Multimodal AI system capable of understanding text, images, and complex reasoning tasks.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: No</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Major competitor to ChatGPT with strong integration across Google services.</span>


<span style="color:#0A66C2;font-weight:600;">Orange Data Mining</span>

- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Country</b>: Slovenia (University of Ljubljana)</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Purpose</b>: Visual programming platform for machine learning, data mining, and interactive data visualization.</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Open Source</b>: Yes</span>  
- <span style="color:#444;"><b>Comparison</b>: Easier for beginners compared to coding-based machine learning libraries like Scikit-Learn.</span>
